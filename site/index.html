    About

    Welcome to my little space on the internet! I hope you enjoy your stay.

    My name is Basil and I love stories.

    Here is where I share audiobooks I have created for some of my favourite stories. By reading (or listening) we learn about times long gone or that never existed in our material plane, but we also learn about ourselves and the present moment. We live in unique times, but what we share across the boundaries of culture and generation is what makes us human, our desires and fears that have been with us since the beginning. I select stories that are in the public domain or that I otherwise have permission to read. This means I may not be able to read some stories published more recently, but luckily there is an abundance of captivating material to choose from.

    I will be releasing more over time, so feel free to bookmark the page and come back.

    I take suggestions for what to read - please keep in mind that all stories must be public domain or have explicit permission from the copyright holder. Short stories are also much quicker to produce. View contact for more details.

    I also maintain a blog here, where I will share updates for the website and other general thoughts.

    The website was designed by me and the code is public source on GitHub.
